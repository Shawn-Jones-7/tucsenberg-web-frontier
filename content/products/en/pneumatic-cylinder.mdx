## Product Overview

The PC-63 Series pneumatic cylinders are engineered to ISO 15552 international standards, ensuring dimensional interchangeability with major global brands. These robust actuators deliver reliable linear motion for automated manufacturing, packaging, and material handling applications.

### Key Features

- **Full ISO 15552 Compliance**: Direct replacement for Festo, SMC, Parker equivalents
- **Precision Machining**: Tight tolerances for smooth, consistent operation
- **Long Service Life**: Tested to 20 million cycles minimum
- **Flexible Configuration**: Extensive options for customization

### Mounting Options

1. **Basic Mounts**
   - Foot mounting (LB)
   - Flange mounting (FA/FB)
   - Clevis mounting (CA/CB)

2. **Pivot Mounts**
   - Trunnion (TC/TM)
   - Swivel eye (I)

3. **Rod End Options**
   - Male thread (M)
   - Female thread (F)
   - Clevis (Y)
   - Spherical bearing (KJ)

### Material Options

**Standard Configuration:**
- Barrel: Anodized aluminum alloy
- End caps: Die-cast aluminum
- Piston rod: Chrome plated carbon steel
- Seals: NBR (nitrile rubber)

**Stainless Steel Version:**
- Barrel: 304 stainless steel
- End caps: 304 stainless steel
- Piston rod: 316 stainless steel
- Seals: FKM (Viton)

### Accessories

- Mounting brackets and feet
- Rod clevis and clevised joints
- Position sensors (magnetic reed, Hall effect)
- Flow control valves
- Quick exhaust valves
- Floating joints

### Ordering Information

**Part Number Format:** PC-[Bore]-[Stroke]-[Options]

Example: PC-63-200-SC-FA-M

- PC-63: 63mm bore
- 200: 200mm stroke
- SC: Stainless cylinder option
- FA: Front flange mount
- M: Magnetic piston for sensors

### Quality Assurance

Every cylinder is tested for:
- Air tightness (internal and external leakage)
- Stroke accuracy
- Operating smoothness
- Cushioning function
- Pressure resistance at 1.5x rated pressure
