## Product Overview

Our SS-316L fastener range delivers exceptional corrosion resistance and mechanical strength for the most demanding environments. Each batch is manufactured from certified raw materials with complete traceability from mill to finished product.

### Key Features

- **Superior Corrosion Resistance**: Molybdenum-enhanced 316L grade for chloride environments
- **Consistent Quality**: Statistical process control on all critical dimensions
- **Full Certification**: Mill test reports and material certificates included
- **Wide Selection**: Comprehensive range of head styles, drive types, and lengths

### Product Range

#### Hex Bolts & Screws
- Hex head cap screws (DIN 933 / ISO 4017)
- Hex head bolts (DIN 931 / ISO 4014)
- Socket head cap screws (DIN 912 / ISO 4762)
- Button head screws (ISO 7380)

#### Nuts
- Hex nuts (DIN 934 / ISO 4032)
- Nyloc nuts (DIN 985)
- Flange nuts (DIN 6923)
- Coupling nuts

#### Washers & Other
- Flat washers (DIN 125 / ISO 7089)
- Spring washers (DIN 127)
- Nord-Lock washers
- Threaded rod and studs
